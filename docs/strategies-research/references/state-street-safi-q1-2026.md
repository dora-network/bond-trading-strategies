# State Street SAFI Q1 2026 Commentary

**URL:** https://www.ssga.com/us/en/institutional/insights/fixed-income-q1-2026-safi-commentary

**Published:** May 2026

## What It Covers

Live performance and methodology of State Street's Systematic Active Fixed Income (SAFI) strategy, covering intermediate (1-10Yr) and long (10+Yr) corporate bond portfolios since December 2023 inception.

## Key Claims

1. **Value, momentum, and sentiment signals** drive security selection — value is the main alpha source in most environments; momentum most powerful during credit weakness *(extracted)*
2. **Factor behavior is complementary** — value and momentum frequently offset each other, contributing to alpha consistency across regimes *(extracted)*
3. **SAFI navigated three credit stress episodes** successfully: Yen carry unwind (Aug 2024), Liberation Day (Apr 2025), Iran War (Mar 2026) *(extracted)*
4. **Long portfolio** delivered 35 bps alpha in March 2026 alone — 9 bps from value, 9 bps from momentum *(extracted)*
5. **Information ratios:** Intermediate portfolio IR of 2.78 (1-year), Long portfolio IR of 1.95 (1-year) *(extracted)*
6. **Systematic continuous rebalancing** captures opportunities from increased dispersion in factor scores during volatile periods *(extracted)*
7. **Security-level attribution** shows right-skewed contribution distribution — 225 tickers contributed positively (42.5 bps), 119 detracted (23.6 bps) *(extracted)*

## Limitations

- Inception only December 2023 — limited track record
- Performance data is gross/net of fees from the manager; no independent audit
- Strategy limited to US IG corporate bonds
