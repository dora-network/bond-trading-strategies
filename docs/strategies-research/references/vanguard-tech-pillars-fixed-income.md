# Vanguard: Tech Pillars Shaping Fixed Income's Future

**URL:** https://corporate.vanguard.com/content/corporatesite/us/en/corp/articles/the-tech-pillars-shaping-fixed-incomes-future.html

**Published:** June 2026

## What It Covers

Vanguard's three-pillar technology framework for fixed income: Enhanced Insights (AI/ML for alpha signals), Faster Decisions (optimization engines), and Optimized Execution (liquidity aggregation, electronic trading).

## Key Claims

1. **>2/3 of Treasury trading** and nearly 50% of IG corporate trading now electronic *(extracted)*
2. **AI analyzes thousands of earnings calls**, news, and bond documents — expanding fundamental analysis to 5,000+ IG issuers *(extracted)*
3. **ETF basket creation** reduced from 2 hours to under 10 minutes via optimization engines *(extracted)*
4. **Liquidity Aggregator** processes ~4M dealer quotes/day for munis, generating ~100 actionable trades — improving intraday fair-value pricing *(extracted)*
5. **Quantum computing collaboration** with IBM for portfolio optimization — early models demonstrated improved outcomes vs. classical methods *(extracted)*
6. **Human-in-the-loop philosophy** — AI-driven insights guided by experienced PMs; technology extends human expertise, not replaces it *(extracted)*
7. **Prepayment model** for MBS using advanced ML to capitalize on market inefficiencies *(extracted)*

## Limitations

- Vanguard perspective — may present their approach as more advanced than competitors
- Performance claims are internal; no independent verification
- Quantum computing benefits are still research-phase
